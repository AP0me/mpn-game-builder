## Gadgets
### Weapons and Shields
These gadgets enhance your ability to inflict or resist harm, often with a specific thematic flavor.
- **Kinetic Weapon/Armor:** Brute force, physical impact, physical resilience. Think guns, railguns or heavy plate.
- **Energy Weapon/Shield:** Plasma beams, cryo weapon, flamethrower, plasma-ablative shields.
- **Sonic Weapon/Shield:** Uses high-frequency sound waves to destroy targets or projects a concentrated wall of sound waves to deflect.
- **Optical Weapon/Shield:** Lasers, blinding light, reflectors.
- **Electro-magnetic Weapon/Shield:** EMPs, repulsion shields.
- **Gravitational Weapon/Shield:** Force fields, gravitational pulse guns.
- **Chemical Weapon:** Acid, corrosives, irritants.
- **Bio Weapon:** Pathogens, engineered viruses, gene modifiers.
- **Ionizing Weapon:** Radiation emitters, particle accelerators.
- **Antimatter Weapon/Shield:** Antimatter beams, antimatter-ablative shields.
### Space-Time
These gadgets bend space-time, offering extraordinary capabilities.
- **Telekinesis Device:** A device that allows the user to move objects with their mind.
- **Time machine:** A device that allows the user to subtly speed up or slow down local time.
- **Stasis Field** A device that creates a "bubble" where time effectively stops.
### Transportation
These gadgets allow for unprecedented mobility and access.
- **Portal Gun** A device capable of creating two connected wormholes, allowing for instant travel between them.
- **Teleporter:** A device capable of instantly moving the objects and creatures across vast distances.
- **Space Ship:** A vessel designed for interstellar travel.
- **Submarine:** A vessel for deep-sea exploration.
- **Air Ship:** A large, low energy, aerial vessel for transport.
- **Tunnel Boring Machine:** A massive machine designed to excavate tunnels.
- **Winged Jet Suit:** A personal flight system, offering agile aerial mobility.
- **Space Elevator** A structure connecting a planet's surface to orbit, allowing for fuel-efficient transport.
- **Hyperloop:** A sealed tube system for high-speed, frictionless transport.
- **Grappling Hook:** A device launching a hook and line for rapid vertical transportation.
### Communication
These gadgets bridge distances and minds, or sever connections.
- **Ansible:** A device for faster-than-light communication across any distance.
- **Universal Translator:** A device that instantly translates any language, spoken or written.
- **Signal Jammer:** A device that disrupts electronic communications and signals in an area.
### Computers
These gadgets represent the cutting edge of information and automation.
- **Quantum Computer:** A computer using qubits to perform complex calculations at speeds impossible for binary systems (e.g., breaking encryption instantly).
- **AI Assistant:** A sophisticated artificial intelligence, capable of processing information, providing advice, and managing systems.
- **Heads-Up Display (HUD)** A transparent display that presents data.
- **Distant Controller:** A device for remotely operating machinery or systems.
- **Simulation:** A device capable of creating highly realistic virtual environments for training, analysis, or deception.
- **Hologram:** A device projecting three-dimensional light constructs.
- **Robot:** An autonomous or remotely controlled mechanical servant or combatant.
- **Drone:**: A flying robot.
- **Nanobots:** Microscopic robots capable of self-assembly, repair, or infiltration.
### Materials
These gadgets represent exotic or highly specialized substances.
- **Energy Matter:** Matter composed or infused with raw energy, highly unstable and potent.
- **Anti-matter:** The ultimate fuel or weapon, annihilating upon contact with normal matter.
- **Graphene:** A super-strong, super-conductive, and incredibly light material.
- **Radioactive Matter:** Unstable isotopes that emit radiation.
- **Superconductor:** A material that conducts electricity with zero resistance, at room temrature.
- **Explosive:** A material designed for rapid, destructive expansion.
- **Reagent:** A chemical substance that easily reacts with most materials.
- **Aerogel:** An ultra-light, highly insulative, and often translucent material.
- **Magnet:** A powerful magnetic field generator.
- **Ferrofluid:** A liquid that becomes strongly magnetized in the presence of a magnetic field.
- **Universal Fuel:** A highly potent and useable energy source.
### BioChemical
These gadgets interact directly with biological systems.
- **Anti-biotic:** A powerful medicine to combat infections.
- **Pain killer:** A potent analgesic that suppresses physical pain.
- **Steroid:** A performance-enhancing drug that boosts physical capabilities.
- **Mutagen:** A substance that induces controlled genetic mutations.
- **Poison:** A toxic substance designed to cause harm by distrupting biochemical processes.
- **Anti-venom:** A specific antidote to a particular venom or toxin.
- **Hormone Synthesizer:** A device that releases chemicals to subtly influences the target's behavior.
- **Regenerative Serum:** A compound that vastly accelerates the body's natural healing factor for a short duration.
### Energy
These gadgets are about power generation and storage.
- **Reactor:** A compact, high-output energy generator (e.g., fusion, fission, anti-matter).
- **Wireless Power Transmitter:** A system that beams electrical energy to devices without the need for cables or batteries.
- **ZPM:** A device that extracts vacuum energy from a localized region of subspace-time.
- **Dyson Swarm:** A megastructure of orbiting satellites around a star to capture its power output.
- **Generator:** A portable or localized power generation unit (e.g., combustion, geothermal).
- **Solar Panels:** Highly efficient panels that convert sunlight into energy.
- **Battery:** An advanced energy storage unit, far beyond conventional capacities.
### Sensor
These gadgets extend and enhance perception beyond normal human limits.
- **X-Ray Vision:** A device allowing the user to see through solid objects.
- **Night Vision:** A camera allowing the user to see in complete darkness.
- **Thermal Vision:** A camera allowing the user to see thermal signatures.
- **Atmospheric:** A device that analyzes air composition, pressure, and other atmospheric conditions.
- **Geiger Counter:** A device for detecting ionizing radiation.
- **Spectrometer:** A device that analyzes the light spectrum of materials to identify their composition.
- **Biometric:** A device that identifies individuals based on unique biological markers (fingerprints, retinal scans, DNA).
- **Radar:** A device using radio waves to detect range, speed, and direction of objects.
- **Sonar:** A device using sound waves to detect range, speed, and direction of objects.
- **GPS:** A highly accurate global positioning and navigation system.
- **Electro-Reception:** A sensor that detects the weak bio-electric fields generated by living things.
### Biomechatronics
These gadgets merge biology and machinery, often directly interfacing with the user.
- **Stasis Chamber** Devices for long-term preservation of living beings.
- **Nutrient Synthesizer** A device that can create edible substance from carbon and water.
- **Med-bay** Automated medical station capable of diagnosing and treating a wide range of ailments and injuries.
- **Hypnosis Device:** A device that induces a hypnotic state for suggestion.
- **Thought Decrypter:** A device that can interpret and translate brainwaves into coherent thoughts or data.
- **Brain Chip:** A neural implant that enhances cognitive abilities, memory, and allows direct digital interface.
- **Neural Jack:** A port installed in the body allowing direct cable connection to computers.
- **Prostetic Organ:** A synthetic replacement for a biological organ, often with enhanced capabilities.
- **Exoskeleton:** A powered external frame that enhances strength, durability, and mobility.
- **Hazmat Suit:** A suit providing protection against hazardous materials.
- **Space Suit:** A suit for survival and operation in the vacuum of space.
- **Phase Suit:** A suit capable of rendering the user fully intangible, allowing them to pass through solid objects.
- **Active Camouflage/Cloak:** A suit or device that matches color to its environment, rendering the wearer nearly invisible.
